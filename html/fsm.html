FlightStrip Manager

As an ATC on an online network like IVAO, VATSIM or POSCON, have you ever lost track during the coordination process? Usually, for example as a tower controller, you are not only responsible for arrivals and departures, but also for movements on the ground and the start approvals. Here it can quickly happen that you lose track of which pilot has already received his take-off, taxi or take-off clearance. With the FlightStrip Manager you can set up your own individually designed virtual controller workplace and keep track of everything - directly in your browser, no installation required!

Easily add and remove columns

The FlightStrip Manager has a dynamic system to add and remove columns. This allows you to map any airport in the world, regardless of whether it is called Ground or Apron, or how many stations the airport has. Customize your workspace to match your controlling needs perfectly.

Real-time collaboration with other controllers

Our multiplayer feature offers the possibility to connect and collaborate with other controllers in real-time. All changes are synchronized instantly between all connected users, allowing seamless teamwork during busy traffic situations. Perfect for larger airports with multiple active positions.

Connect to online networks

Fetch live data directly from VATSIM, IVAO, and POSCON servers. Automatically import aircraft information, flight plans, and current positions to quickly populate your flight strips without manual data entry.

Automatic proximity-based strip creation

Flight strips are automatically added when an aircraft enters a specified radius around your configured airport. Works for all traffic types - arrivals, departures, and VFR. Define your proximity range and let the system populate your workspace automatically.

Fully customizable keyboard shortcuts

Customize the keyboard shortcuts to your preferences for maximum efficiency. Control aids can be switched on and off as needed. Configure the application to match your workflow and controlling style perfectly.

Ready to enhance your ATC experience?

Launch the FlightStrip Manager web app now and start controlling with a modern, browser-based solution. No downloads, no installation - just open and control!
