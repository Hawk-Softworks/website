Open WebApp

FlightPlanner

With the FlightPlanner, simple flight plans can be created quickly. This ensures a good overview during a flight on e.g. IVAO or VATSIM. All important information needed during the route is clearly displayed on one page.

SimBrief and live METAR

The last flight plan created on SimBrief can be integrated with one click. Using the Fetch METAR buttons, the METAR can be retrieved in real-time.

Frequency-Database and SID/STAR Finder

All available frequencies can be obtained for every airport in the world. Also the current SID and STAR can be calculated based on the airport, the runway and a waypoint. Always with the latest AIRAC Cycle.

Printable Flightplan

The final flight plan can be generated as PDF. You can choose between Light, Normal and Dark Theme. The flight plan is structured according to the different phases of a flight and contains all information, summarized on one page.
