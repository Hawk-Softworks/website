FlightStrip Manager

The FlightStrip Manager is the ideal web-based tool to keep track of your traffic. Each controlled aircraft is displayed as a flight strip and can be edited in real-time together with other air traffic controllers in multiplayer mode.

FlightPlanner

With the FlightPlanner you can create your individual flight plan, where you get all important information about your flight on one DIN A4 page. Access your current SimBrief flight plan, the current weather, an extensive frequency database and the current AIRAC cycle to find the necessary SIDs and STARs.
